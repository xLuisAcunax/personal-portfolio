<ul>
    <li>Lightweight and high-performance <strong>TypeScript animation library</strong> compiled for ESM, CJS, and CDN.</li>
    <li>Unified frame-rate independent <strong>Ticker loop</strong> preventing layout thrashing and paint overhead.</li>
    <li>Advanced features including <strong>Timelines</strong> with relative offsets and interactive <strong>Scroll Binds</strong> (scrubbing).</li>
    <li>Built-in utility modules for typography reveals (<strong>SplitText</strong>) and path tracing (<strong>DrawSVG</strong>).</li>
    <li>Zero external dependencies, built for maximum performance and 60fps rendering.</li>
</ul>
